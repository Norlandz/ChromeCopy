3. The Math of Flattening (The Vectorization)

Each patch is a 3D volume: 

        16 (height)×16 (width)×3 (channels)16 \text{ (height)} \times 16 \text{ (width)} \times 3 \text{ (channels)}16 (height)×16 (width)×3 (channels)
. To feed this into the Transformer, it must be a 1D vector.

The Strict Transformation:

  1. Extract Patch: 

            x(i,j)∈R16×16×3x^{(i,j)} \in \mathbb{R}^{16 \times 16 \times 3}x(i,j)∈R16×16×3

            i,ji,ji,j
    are the grid coordinates 
            (0..13,0..13)(0..13, 0..13)(0..13,0..13)
    .

  2. Flatten:

            16×16×3=76816 \times 16 \times 3 = 76816×16×3=768

            xp(k)x_p^{(k)}xp(k)​

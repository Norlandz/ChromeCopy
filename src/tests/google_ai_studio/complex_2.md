Here is a short demonstration of LaTeX integrated into nested structures:

*   **Mathematical Constants**
    *   Inline form: The ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter is $\pi \approx 3.14159$.
    *   Equation form:
        $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 $$

*   **Calculus Operations**
    1.  **Derivatives:** The power rule states that $\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$.
    2.  **Integrals:** A common definite integral is:
        $$\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$$
    3.  **Limits:** We define continuity using $\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = f(a)$.

*   **Matrices & Sets**
    *   A $2 \times 2$ identity matrix is written as:
        $\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}$
    *   Set notation: $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}, x^2 \geq 0$.
